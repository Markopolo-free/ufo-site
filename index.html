Unidentified Aerial Devices (UADs/UAPs)

NameDescriptionImage
Tic Tac UAPWhite, oblong object recorded by U.S. Navy pilots in 2004; exhibited hypersonic speeds with no visible propulsion.
Black TriangleSilent, massive triangular craft with lights at each corner; widely reported in Belgium (1989) and the U.S.
Phoenix LightsV-shaped formation of lights over Arizona (1997); one of the largest mass sightings in U.S. history.
GoFast ObjectU.S. Navy-released UAP (2015); fast-moving object over ocean, later analyzed in Pentagon reports.
